What we are?

We are Nutripoa Company Limited, a company dedicated in promoting healthy living to people. We specialize in organic composting, promoting healthy living through nutrition and diet guidance, and cultivating fresh, sustainable produce on our farm. Explore our site to discover how we are nurturing the earth, enriching lives and growing a greener future together.

What we do?

We majorly focus on turning organic wastes to manure through various methods as they are elaborated further in our organic hub page. We also guide people on proper ways to handle their diet and take nutrition seriously. Finally we indulge in growing microgreens in our farm using organic methods i.e using our own made compost manure thus ensuring that we provide chemical-free products.
